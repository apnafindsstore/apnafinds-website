APNAFINDS PERFECT WEBSITE V5
============================

WHAT WAS ADDED
--------------
1. COD advance payment:
   - Customer must pay an online advance before placing a COD order.
   - Default amount: ₹49.
   - Seller can change it from ₹10 to ₹49.
   - The remaining balance is shown as payable on delivery.
   - Courier booking waits until advance payment is paid or verified.

2. Seller Account page:
   http://localhost:3000/admin-seller.html

3. Payments page:
   http://localhost:3000/admin-payments.html

4. Full Customer Details page:
   http://localhost:3000/admin-customers.html

CUSTOMER PASSWORDS
------------------
Passwords are intentionally not visible to the seller or administrator.
A secure store must never show customer passwords, OTPs, UPI PINs or card
details. Use password reset instead.

PAYMENT DETAILS YOU CAN SEE
---------------------------
- Payment method
- COD advance required
- Advance amount paid
- Payment status
- Transaction/reference ID
- Balance payable on delivery
- Order total
- Payment date

HOW TO START
------------
1. Extract the ZIP.
2. Double-click START-APNAFINDS.bat.
3. Keep the black window open.
4. Open http://localhost:3000.

ADMIN LOGIN
-----------
Email: admin@apnafinds.local
Password: ApnaFinds@123

DEFAULT COD ADVANCE
-------------------
₹49 in demo automatic-payment mode.

Change it in Admin Seller Account or in .env:
COD_ADVANCE_AMOUNT=49
COD_ADVANCE_MODE=demo

REAL PAYMENT WARNING
--------------------
Demo mode simulates payment locally. It does not transfer real money.
For real advance collection, connect Razorpay, Cashfree, PhonePe, PayU or
another gateway and verify payments server-side.

Your exact Index page remains unchanged.
