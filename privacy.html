Privacy Policy

Learn what information the website may collect and how it should be handled.

Information Collected

The website may collect customer names, email addresses, mobile numbers, delivery addresses, account information, order details and support messages.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to process orders, provide delivery updates, manage customer accounts, respond to support requests, prevent misuse and improve the shopping experience.

Current Testing Storage

This development version stores account and order information in the browser using localStorage. Before launch, customer information must be moved to a secure backend with suitable access controls and retention practices.
