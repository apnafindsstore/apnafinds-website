Cancellation

An order may be eligible for cancellation before dispatch. Use the Returns & Cancellations page to submit a request. Approval depends on the current fulfilment status.

Returns and Replacements

Delivered products may be reviewed for return or replacement when damaged, incorrect, defective or materially different from the description. Keep the product, packaging, invoice and accessories until the request is resolved.

Refund Review

Submitting a request does not automatically guarantee approval. Refund value, method and timing depend on the review, payment method, product condition and fulfilment partner.

Review this draft policy with your suppliers, courier partner and payment provider before launching the store.
